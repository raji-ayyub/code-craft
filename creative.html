Color Usage Guidelines

Best practices for applying colors in your designs

Color Hierarchy

Establish a clear visual hierarchy by using your primary color for main actions, secondary color for secondary actions, and accent colors for highlights and notifications.

Accessibility

Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background colors. Use tools to check contrast ratios to make your designs accessible to everyone.

Consistency

Maintain consistency in your color usage across all components and screens. Create a style guide to document your color choices.
